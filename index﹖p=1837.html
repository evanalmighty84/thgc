Life Insurance is Only for the Elderly

Risk Factors

Life insurance is only necessary for older individuals nearing retirement.

Solution

Life insurance benefits individuals of all ages, especially those with dependents, mortgages, or significant debt.

Life Insurance is Too Expensive

Risk Factors

Life insurance is too costly and unaffordable for the average person.

Solution

Life insurance can be quite affordable, with various policy options to fit different budgets.

Employer-Provided Life Insurance is Sufficient

Risk Factors

The life insurance my employer provides is enough to cover all my needs.

Solution

Employer-provided life insurance is often limited and may not cover all financial obligations.

Single People Dont Need Life Insurance

Risk Factors

Single individuals without dependents do not need life insurance.

Solution

Single people can benefit from life insurance to cover debts and funeral expenses and leave a financial legacy to loved ones.

Life Insurance Payouts are Taxable

Risk Factors

The payout from a life insurance policy is subject to income tax.

Solution

Life insurance payouts are generally not taxable, providing a tax-free benefit to the beneficiaries.
